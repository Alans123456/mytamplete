Welcome to Hotel sauraha

Best budget hotel in sauraha

Hotel Sauraha is a renowned establishment located in Sauraha, a charming town in the Chitwan District of Nepal. Situated near the Chitwan National Park, one of Nepal’s most famous conservation areas, Hotel Sauraha offers a unique blend of natural beauty and comfortable accommodation, making it a preferred destination for both domestic and international travelers. The hotel prides itself on providing exceptional hospitality and a serene environment that allows guests to unwind and connect with nature. The architectural design of Hotel Sauraha harmoniously blends traditional Nepali aesthetics with modern amenities, offering a comfortable and culturally enriching stay. The rooms are well-appointed, featuring modern conveniences such as air conditioning, en-suite bathrooms, Wi-Fi, and private balconies that offer stunning views of the lush surroundings.
